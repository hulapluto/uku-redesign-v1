About

Hey there! I've been doing web design freelance and for companies for over 20 years now. I got interested in web design back in the 90's, the AOL days. If you aren't familiar, you aren't missing much. Web pages were much simpler, and after seeing HTML, I thought, "Pffft, I can do that!" and i've been doign it ever since.

My career on the web started when my boss asked me to create a help site for our technical support staff. Then I was promoted to webmaster and began working on the content and design for the corporate site, but also oversaw the web-hosting department. I was solely responsible for the web-hosting server, customer data, dns and even helping them with perl scripting and HTML issues.

The last few roles I've held have been managing content for corporate sites, so focus on quality is a must. Although my career specialty has been Front-End Design, I've been able to seamlessly integrate skills from my interest in recording Music to provide Audio and Video production expertise, something companies typically go to agencies for.
